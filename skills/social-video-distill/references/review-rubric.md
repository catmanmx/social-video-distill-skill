# Content Review Rubric

Use this rubric after transcripts are generated.

## Output Shape

For each item, record:

- title or ID
- usable as-is / needs light edit / needs fact-check / unusable until re-listened
- logic notes
- likely transcription mistakes
- factual or compliance risks
- reusable ideas
- suggested rewrite direction

## Common Risk Categories

- Platform rules: treat as time-sensitive. Mark as "needs current official verification" unless a current official source is provided.
- Algorithms: avoid presenting inferred ranking signals as official formulas.
- Monetization: mark thresholds, payouts, and program availability as time-sensitive.
- Tax/legal/finance: do not present as advice. Require official or professional sources.
- Copyright/IP: flag recognizable characters, logos, music, screenshots, or copied scripts.

## Language Cleanup

Prefer:

- "可能", "通常", "从公开规则看", "从创作者经验看"

Avoid:

- "官方算法就是", "一定", "保证", "必然", "永久有效", "完美方案"
